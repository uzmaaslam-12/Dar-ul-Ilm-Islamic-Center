Let’s Learn Basic Islamic Knowledge

In this course, your child will not only learn Noorani Qaida but also develop essential Islamic values. Our tutors dedicate around 20% of each class to nurturing good character, strong morals, obedience, and honesty in students. We focus on providing basic Islamic knowledge, including

  • Basic Aqaa’id (beliefs) about Allah, Angels, Holy Books, and Prophets
  • MThe Pillars of Islam.
  • Step-by-step method of performing Wudu (Ablution)
  • Practice and understanding of five daily prayers (Salah)
  • Memorization of: Short Surahs, Six Kalimaat, Imaan-e-Mufassil & Mujmal, Ayat-ul-Kursi, Dua-e-Qunoot etc
  • Masnoon Duas for daily life
  • Islamic manners and etiquette
  • Engaging Islamic and moral stories for kids
