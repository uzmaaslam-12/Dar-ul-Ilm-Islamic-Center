About Dar-ul-Ilm Islamic Center

Dar-ul-Ilm Islamic Center helps students with Quran recitation and is a premier platform to learn Quran online through expert guidance and flexible schedules. As a leading online Quran academy, we offer professional online Tajweed classes and a complete online Tajweed course to help students recite the Quran with accuracy and confidence. Our experienced online Quran tutors provide personalized learning, along with Quran Tafseer classes online to understand the true meaning of the Quran. We also offer a Quran translation course in Urdu, including Quran word by word translation and complete Quran translation in Urdu for deeper understanding. In addition, students can enroll in our online Islamic courses, or strengthen their language skills with our Arabic language course, which includes learn Arabic online programs and interactive Arabic language classes for better comprehension of the Quran.
